Frequently Asked Questions

What is In-Flight Madness?

• In-Flight Madness is designed to be a flight attendant simulation game where you must expect the unexpected. Whether it's a passenger asking for a cup of coffee or the pilot having a medical emergency, you must be prepared for anything.

When and where will the game release?

• In-Flight Madness will be released on Steam either later this year or in 2025, depending on the feedback during the beta testing process.

I'm a beta tester, what should I do?

• If you've been selected for beta testing, you should play the game and give us feedback about it. What went well? What didn't? Let us know by sending us an email or by contacting us on our social media (links to both of these are at the bottom).

How do I become a beta tester?

• We typically select aviation enthusiasts for beta testing. You'll know you've been selected when you see an invitation for beta testing in your email inbox. Some beta testers also recieve financial rewards as a gift for helping us develop our game.

I get the "Invalid resource ID" error when trying to install the game, what should I do?

• If you're getting this error and you've been selected for beta testing, we likely goofed up and forgot to authorize you. Contact us using the links at the bottom of the page and we will try to authorize you, if you've recieved a valid invitation that is.

I get the "Invalid resource ID" error when trying to install the game and I haven't been invited to test the game, what should I do?

• If you're getting this error and you haven't been invited for beta testing, you must recieve an invitation first in order to install the game. Thus, when you try and install IFM without an invitation, the "Invalid resource ID" error pops up (or we just forgot to authorize you for beta testing if you were invited)
