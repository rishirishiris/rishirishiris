Beta testing and sponsorships now available!

Hello everyone! As mentioned in the previous blog by my fellow dev Hristo, we would give out new info about the beta testing application process and release soon, in another blog post. And here we are! After some preparations, we are launching the beta testing program today. If you're interested, shoot one of us devs an email and you might be accepted! Also being launched today is the YouTube sponsorship program! We will select various YouTubers that we would think would make great beta testers and invite them to test our game and additionally, make a video reviewing it on their channel in exchange for a financial reward. If you're an aviation YouTuber, check your inbox! You might've been invited and can earn money simply by sending us feedback and making a review video! Anyway, that's all for this post. Check this page frequently for more updates about the development proccess.
