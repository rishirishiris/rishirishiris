Blog 3: New In-Flight Madness Social Media!

By: Simeon (notthetsar@inflightmadness.com) on September 24th, 2024

As our beta testers are working hard and as our game is getting closer and closer to release, we have decided to create various social media accounts so the upcoming playerbase has somewhere to interact with other players, as well as adding another option to communicate with us developers aside from e-mail. As of September 24th, 2024, only Instagram, Twitter, and YouTube acounts have been created, but we are planning on adding more after the game's release. On each account, we will primarily post the following: YouTube- On YouTube we will post promotional material such as trailers, as well as things like tutorials, designed to help the playerbase out with learning the game. Twitter- On Twitter (X) you will find all sorts of general announcements about the game, such as new updates, the development process, and all sorts of important announcents Instagram- On Instagram, we will post screenshots and promotional material for the game, so more users find out about us. Anyways, that's all for today's blog. Be sure to follow each of our social media by clicking the newly added links to them at the bottom of the page. Be sure to check this blog page frequently for more updates about the game and its development.
